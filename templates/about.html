About MagnetBank

What is this?

At MagnetBank, we understand the importance of privacy and security when it comes to accessing torrent metadata. That's why we only store the hash, filename, and announce URL of each torrent. This means that there are no actual torrents or magnet links stored on our platform.

Our links are generated dynamically, ensuring that users have access to the most up-to-date announce URLs from a public list.
